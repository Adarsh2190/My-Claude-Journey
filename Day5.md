Most people use AI wrong. Here's what I learned today. 👇

I compared two AI prompts for building a 30-day learning roadmap:

With NO context → Generic output. Vague tasks. Resources you could Google yourself.

With CONTEXT → Personalized plan. Matched to my skills, schedule, goal, and learning style.

Here's the exact context that made the difference:
✅ Current role (Student / Professional / Freelancer)
✅ Existing skills
✅ Target goal
✅ Hours available per day
✅ Experience level
✅ Preferred learning style (Videos / Projects / Reading)

The more specific your input, the more valuable the output.
