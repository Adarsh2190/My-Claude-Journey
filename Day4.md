**🤖 AI Prompting**
You learned how to write a powerful **role-based system prompt** — by telling Claude *"You are an Elite AI Career Strategist"*, you got a much more structured and expert response than a plain question would give.

**📋 Structured Information Gathering**
You saw how to make Claude ask **targeted questions first** before generating output, rather than making assumptions. This leads to far more personalized results.

**🧠 Chain-of-Thought Reasoning**
You learned to instruct an AI to *think step by step* — analyze position → find gaps → recommend actions → build milestones — instead of jumping straight to an answer.

**🎨 PDF Generation (Two Approaches)**
- **ReportLab** — drawing elements manually with x/y coordinates. You saw firsthand why this causes overlapping: one miscalculation and everything shifts.
- **WeasyPrint (HTML → PDF)** — the better approach. Write clean HTML/CSS, then convert. Containers handle layout automatically, so nothing overlaps.

**📐 Layout Design Principles**
You learned why **card-based layouts** (each section in its own box) are more reliable than absolute positioning for documents.

**🔁 Iterative Prompting**
You practiced giving **feedback to refine output** ("it's still overlapping") — a key skill when working with AI tools to get production-quality results.
